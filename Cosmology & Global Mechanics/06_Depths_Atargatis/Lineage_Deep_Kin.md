# Lineage: Deep Kin (The Abiding)

## The Ti-Abzu (Ancient Depth-Dwellers)
**Identity:** Bioluminescent Bodhisattvas.
**Role:** Listeners to the "Breath of the World" (vents).
**Biology:** Massive, pressure-adapted, slow. Perceive "Spirit-Currents."
**Culture:** Time moves slower. Surface life is "Noisy Water."

## The Kur-Datsu (Pressure-Changed)
**Identity:** Descendants of surface-dwellers who went deep.
**Role:** Translators between Surface and Deep.
**Biology:** Compressed, dense, flattened. Cannot ascend fully.
**Culture:** "The Stripping" (Pressure removes the false). Maintain "Sun-Altars" to remember the light they left.
