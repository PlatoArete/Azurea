# Lineage: The En-He-Dao (Way-of-Water Folk)

## Identity
**Role:** Children of the Journey. Guardians of rivers.
**Nature:** Serpentine, river-dwelling, restless.
**Philosophy:** "The river is the sea's becoming."

## Practices
**Canyon-Singing:** Using sound to help water erode stone.
**The Journey:** Life is a flow from mountain to sea.

## Branches
- **Mountain-Born:** Fast, cold streams.
- **Valley-Folk:** Wide rivers, traders.
- **Delta-Dwellers:** Salt-fresh mixing.
- **Underground-Swimmers:** Pale, blind, mysterious.
