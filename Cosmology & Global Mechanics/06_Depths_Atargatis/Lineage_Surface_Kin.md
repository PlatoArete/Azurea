# Lineage: Surface Kin (The Shimmering)

## The Abgal-Tako (Sage-Arms)
**Identity:** Cephalopod sages. "We think eight thoughts at once."
**Role:** Navigators, traders, diplomats. Masters of complexity.
**Culture:** "Way of the Ink" (Truth is fluid).
**Appearance:** 8-armed, color-shifting, soft-bodied.

## The Shio-Gatis (Tide-Changed)
**Identity:** People of the Threshold.
**Role:** Diplomats, intermediaries.
**Biology:** Aquatic at high tide (scales, gills), terrestrial at low tide (skin, lungs).
**Philosophy:** "The boundary is reality." Change is the only honesty.
