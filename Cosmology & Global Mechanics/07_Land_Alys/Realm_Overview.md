# Realm Overview: Land (Alys)

## The Nature of Alys: The Living Land

Alys is the land itself—soil, forest, hill. She is not a distant deity but a present reality.
**Nature:** The Sleeping Goddess. She dreams the spirits.
**Philosophy:** Reciprocity. The land is alive and must be treated as kin, not resource.

## The Spirits (Genii Loci)
Spirits *are* the awareness of places.
- **Grove Spirits:** Forest guardians.
- **Hill Spirits:** Ancient, territorial, often linked to burial mounds.
- **Spring Spirits:** Healing, prophetic.
- **Field Spirits:** Co-operative with respectful farmers.
- **Hearth Spirits:** Domestic guardians (Brownies/Domovoi).
- **Boundary Spirits:** Liminal, dangerous, protective.

## The Five Regional Traditions
1.  **Western (Celtic):** Grove-focused. Geal-sí (Bright) / Dorcha-sí (Dark).
2.  **Eastern (Shinto):** Purity/pollution focus. Kōrei / Anrei.
3.  **Heartland (Slavic):** Field/Forest focus. Svetlo-dukh / Tëmno-dukh.
4.  **Northern (Norse):** Land-wight (Vættir) focus. Ljós-vættr / Myrk-vættr.
5.  **Southern (Egyptian):** River/Oasis focus. Ma'at (Balance).

## Relationships with Other Realms
- **Kasandra:** Sisters of Growth.
- **Ostara:** Partners in the Cycle.
- **Gwenaelle:** The Highland Border.
- **Atargatis:** The Lowland/Coastal Border.
- **Katiari:** The Forgotten Connection (Fae memory).
