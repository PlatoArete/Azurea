# Lineage: Snake-Kin (Uraeun)

## Biology
**Traits:** Serpentine or semi-serpentine. Lidless eyes. Cold-blooded (Threshold-Baskers).
**Senses:** Smell/Taste (Tongue), Ground Vibration (Bone conduction), Hettang (Ley-line sense).

## Social Structure
**The Hold:** Individual or pair tending a specific threshold.
**Coil-Cluster:** Small group sharing a region.
**Confluence:** Large periodic gathering for mating/news.

## Life Cycle
**Shedding:** Central to growth.
**Mating:** For transformation-cycles, not life.
**Reproduction:** Eggs laid at thresholds; communal raising.

## Relations
**Solarian Church:** Persecuted as "deceivers."
**Revnan:** Natural allies (shadow/hidden).
**Atargatan:** Shared water-thresholds.
