# Lineage: Dragon-Kin (The Hegemony)

## The Inner Fire
Unlike other reptilians, Dragon-kin are warm-blooded (endothermic) via magical/biological "Inner Fire."

## The Three Houses (Lineages)
These function as both biological sub-species and social castes.

### Lóng-Imperiales
**Role:** Sword and Scroll (Admin/Military).
**Appearance:** Metallic scales (Gold, Bronze). Large, armored build.
**Language:** Sino-Romano.

### Tiān-Phanes
**Role:** Star and Crucible (Philosophy/Science).
**Appearance:** Iridescent/Gem-like scales. Slender, horned.
**Language:** Sino-Hellenic.

### Mush-Nāga
**Role:** Root and Ziggurat (Priesthood/Memory).
**Appearance:** Earth-tone, stone-like scales. Massive, ancient.
**Language:** Sanskrit-Sumerian.
