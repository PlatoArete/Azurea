# Lineage: The Fae (First Peoples)

## Identity
Indigenous to the land before mortal races arrived.
**Nature:** Diverse, magical, tied to the land's memory.
**Relationship to Humans:** Fascinated by the "Children of the Forgotten Sister" (Katiari). Tricksters and teachers.

## Dispositions
1.  **Favorably Disposed:** Geal-sí / Kōrei. Aid the respectful.
2.  **Indifferent/Hostile:** Dorcha-sí / Anrei. Dangerous, unconcerned with mortals.
3.  **Balanced:** Scáth-sí / Kagami-rei. Mirrors of mortal behavior.

## Types
- **Tall Ones:** Noble Fae (Sidhe).
- **Small Folk:** Brownies, Pixies.
- **Wild Ones:** Green Men, Tree-folk.
- **Shapeshifters:** Selkies, Púca.
