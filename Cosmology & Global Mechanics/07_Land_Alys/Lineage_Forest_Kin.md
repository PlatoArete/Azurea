# Lineage: Forest Kin (The Attuned)

## Forest Elves
**Nature:** Elves adapted to deep woodlands.
**Biology:** Earthy scent, sensitive eyes/ears, twilight activity.
**Spirituality:** They don't *practice* tradition; they *embody* it.
- **Connection:** Perceptive of spirits; feel the forest's pain as their own.
- **Role:** Mediators between humans and the deep wild.
