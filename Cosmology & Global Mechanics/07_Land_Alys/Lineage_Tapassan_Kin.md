# Lineage: Tapassan Kin (Lizard & Croc Folk)

## Shared Traits
**Cold-Blooded:** Dependent on external warmth (Tapas).
**Sun-Basking:** Biological need elevated to spiritual practice.

## The Lizard-Kin (The Adaptable)
**Nature:** Diverse, scattered, adaptable to any environment.
**Types:** Desert, Forest, Mountain, Swamp, Urban.
**Smaller Kindred:** Kobolds, Kappa-types.
**Culture:** Loose "Basking Communities."

## The Croc-Kin (The Wardens)
**Nature:** River-guardians, powerful, patient.
**Duality:** Need both Sun (warmth) and Water (habitat).
**Culture:** "River-Holds" led by Wardens.
**Reputation:** Unfairly maligned as monsters; actually stewards of waterways.
