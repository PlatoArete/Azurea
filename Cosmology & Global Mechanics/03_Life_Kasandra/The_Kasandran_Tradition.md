# The Kasandran Tradition: Ways of the Living Flame

## The Two Paths

Complementary approaches often practiced in rhythm (Ecstasy for festivals, Equilibrium for daily life).

### 1. The Path of Ecstasy (Dionysian)
**Core:** Life overflows limits. Dissolve the self to let the goddess flow through.
**Practices:**
- **Sacred Intoxication:** Wine/substances as sacraments.
- **Sacred Dance:** Possession through movement.
- **Sacred Sexuality:** Union as worship.
- **The Revel:** Controlled chaos to release pressure.

### 2. The Path of Equilibrium (Epicurean)
**Core:** Pleasure is good, but balance is better. Sustainable flourishing over peak intensity.
**Practices:**
- **Hedonic Calculus:** Will this pleasure lead to pain?
- **Ataraxia:** The undisturbed mind; contentment.
- **Simple Pleasures:** Food, drink, rest, connection.

## Theology

**Seven Principles:**
1. Life Seeks Life (Desire is holy).
2. Pleasure is Wisdom (Body knows what it needs).
3. Creation is Participation.
4. Sharing Multiplies.
5. Bodies Are Sacred.
6. Change is Life.
7. Balance Sustains.
