# Shadow Lineages: Children of the Dark

## The Urkhani (The "Orc" Family)

**Meaning:** "The Deep-Knowers"
**Human Slur:** Orc, Troll
**Nature:** Philosopher-Hunters. Semi-nomadic, following seasonal circuits between sacred caves.
**Aesthetics:** Natural stone, bone, crystal, star motifs.

**Sub-Peoples:**
- **Urkhani-Mûr (Trolls):** Huge, elder, wise.
- **Urkhani (Orcs):** The standard "Seekers".
- **Urkhani-Val (Hobgoblins):** Human-sized diplomats.
- **Urkhani-Ket (Goblins):** Small independent scouts.
- **Urkhani-Zhi (Gremlins):** Tiny, unseen.

**Culture:**
- **Star-Lore:** The night sky is their library. Oral tradition is vast.
- **Hunting:** A spiritual practice of patience.
- **Humor:** They find human fear of the dark hilarious ("You're afraid of your own bed?").

## The Daevani (The "Imp" / "Tiefling" Family)

**Meaning:** "Divine Beings" / "Shining Ones"
**Human Slur:** Demon
**Nature:** Born of shadow. Darkness is natural to them.
**Appearance:** Horns, tails, wings (some), unusual skin colors (red, purple, blue).
**Aesthetics:** "Gothic with a Twist" — inverted spires reaching for stars, stained glass visible only at night, shadow as structure.

**Culture:**
- **Dream-Walking:** Natural ability to navigate dream-space.
- **Art:** Architecture, music (minor key), sculpture.
- **Identity:** They are not fallen angels; they are a distinct people.

## The Ferani (The Beast-Kin)

**Meaning:** "The Wild Ones"
**Nature:** People with animal features (NOT shape-shifters).
**Types:** Feline, Canine, Corvid, Serpentine, etc.
**Aesthetics:** Persian-influenced. Gardens (Paradaeza) that are functional habitats. Vertical architecture for climbing/flying types.

**Relationship to Dark:**
Nocturnal types (cats, bats, owls) align closely with Revna. Others may lean toward Alys (Land) or Ostara (Death - especially Corvids).
