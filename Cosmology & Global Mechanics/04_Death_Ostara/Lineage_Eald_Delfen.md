# Lineage: The Eald-Delfen (Ancient Delvers)

**Human Name:** Deep Dwarves
**Sacred Vocation:** Bānhuysers (Bone-Housers)

## Identity
Dwarves who went deep instead of high. They found the "bones of the earth."
**Appearance:** Pale skin, stocky build, dark-adapted eyes.
**Culture:** Cities are ancestor-libraries.

## The Bānhuyser Craft
Creating "Second Houses" (spirit vessels) for willing ancestors.
- **Materials:** Ceramic, bone, stone.
- **Philosophy:** The body is just the first house. If you wish to stay, we build you another.
- **Relation to Church:** Seen as necromancers. In reality, it is consensual service.
