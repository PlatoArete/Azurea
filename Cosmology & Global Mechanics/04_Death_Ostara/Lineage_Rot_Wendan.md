# Lineage: The Rot-Wendan (Decay-Turners)

**Human Name:** Mushroom Men / Eaters of Sin
**Sacred Vocation:** Cleansers

## Identity
The immune system of the Underworld. Symbiotic with fungi.
**Appearance:** Fungal features, damp eyes, slow movement. Bioluminescent potential.
**Culture:** Collective memory ("The Whole").

## The Work
Consuming spiritual corruption.
- **Not Rot:** They don't cause rot; they *eat* the bad rot (pollution/evil) and turn it into neutral energy.
- **The Blooming:** Erupting in light to cleanse a corrupted area.
- **Symbiosis:** Eald-Delfen make them pots; they keep the tunnels spiritually clean.
