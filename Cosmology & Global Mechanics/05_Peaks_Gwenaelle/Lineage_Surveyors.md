# Lineage: The Surveyors (Power Above)

**Note:** These races were devastated by The Passing.

## The Aetherwings (Sky-Whisperers)
**Role:** Weather-readers. Translators of the sky.
**State:** Traumatized. Many "heard" the Passing and broke.
**Appearance:** Translucent, wind-riding, hair moves with unfelt breeze.

## The Nephilim (Cloud-Walkers)
**Role:** Cloud-herders. Rain-bringers.
**State:** Grounded. Their cloud-cities were destroyed. Survivors fell to the peaks.
**Appearance:** Large, soft-featured, cloudy/gaseous bodies.

## The Sythkarai (Chitinous Weavers)
**Role:** Architects. Weavers of silk and structure.
**State:** Trying to rebuild. Weaving "grieving architecture" (heavier, anchored).
**Appearance:** Insectoid, iridescent chitin. Six-limbed (2 weaving arms).
